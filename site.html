CITIZEN MANAGEMENT SYSTEM





The project 'CITIZEN MANAGEMENT SYSTEM' provides us all the data regarding the citizens of the country. Every citizen has a unique ID to trace the personal as well as professional details of the citizen.

Using this database, it will be easy for the government to govern all kinds of activity of each and every citizen of the country. Citizens can register for several services provided by the government and be a part of the programs initiated by the government and get notified about ucoming events regularly using this system. This database will also enable the government to keep an eye over the income and expenditure of the citizen and keep track of income tax paid by the citizen.

In a huge democracy like India, it is very difficult to manage every thing on pen and paper, hence this kind of database increases the efficiency and decreases the workload of the government and the retrieval of data becomes handy and safer. Hence it is the need of the hour.This system motivates the citizens to obtain the unique ID card and get themselves benefitted by the various amenities provided by the government.
